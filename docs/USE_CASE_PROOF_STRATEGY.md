# Use-case proof strategy (no fake social proof)

LeadIntel’s public proof is **inspectable mechanics**, not fabricated logos or ROI.

## Allowed proof sources

- No-signup sample digest workflow
- Explainable scoring methodology (visible reasons)
- Templates library + token glossary (curly tokens only)
- Competitive report sources & freshness (citations and timestamps)
- Webhook + exports behavior (Team)
- Trust center pages buyers can inspect

## How to present proof by motion

Use-case framing should map to real product surfaces:

- **Account-based outbound**: watchlist → shortlist → account explainability → draft → action
- **Timing-first teams**: freshness, recency, momentum, and why-now summaries
- **RevOps/Enablement**: approvals, audit logs, standardized templates, operator-safe exports

## Prohibited proof

- Fake case studies
- Fake testimonials
- Fake customer logos
- Unverifiable performance metrics
- Enterprise certifications not implemented/documented

# Follow-through intelligence

Follow-through intelligence answers: **is a promising account actually being worked**, and what is blocking progress.

## Labels

- `ready_to_act`
- `needs_follow_through`
- `blocked`
- `waiting_on_review`
- `waiting_on_stronger_signal`
- `stale`

## Data sources

Observed workflow signals:

- action queue items and statuses
- deliveries and failures
- manual review states (when present)

## What it does not claim

- it does not assert rep performance
- it does not infer hidden activity
- it does not claim revenue impact

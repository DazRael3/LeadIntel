# Observed vs inferred

LeadIntel separates what is **observed** from what is **inferred** in planning and revenue guidance.

## Observed examples

- signal events (from stored trigger events)
- momentum labels derived from events
- first-party intent match summaries (when present)
- action queue activity and deliveries
- explicit outcomes entered by users

## Inferred examples

- suggested stakeholder path/persona ordering
- suggested multi-touch sequencing guidance
- workflow “what next” recommendations

## Why this matters

Revenue teams need to understand:

- what’s a real observed signal vs a suggestion
- what evidence is missing
- what could make a recommendation wrong

# Product-led proof (what we can claim publicly)

LeadIntel’s proof system is built from **real mechanics** in the product and public docs.

## Allowed proof sources

- Deterministic, explainable scoring (reasons-based, not black-box ranking)
- Sample preview workflow (no fake customer claims required)
- Action layer: briefs, exports, webhooks (where enabled and entitled)
- Trust center and policy visibility (/trust, /security, /privacy, etc.)
- Honest compare methodology (tradeoffs are explicit)

## Prohibited proof

Do not add:
- fake logos or testimonials
- fabricated customer results or metrics
- unsupported enterprise certifications (SOC 2, ISO 27001, SSO/SAML/SCIM) unless fully true
